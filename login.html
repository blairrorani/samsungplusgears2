Get Started

Not a registered Backstage user? Just sign up for a new account below.

  1. Fill out the New Account form with your details.
  2. An email will immediately be sent to you. (Don’t forget to check your junk mailbox).
  3. Open the email, and then click on the web link.
  4. That will confirm your account and you’ll be logged in. If you have any problems with the above instruction or with logging in then please email support@samsungbackstage.co.uk and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
